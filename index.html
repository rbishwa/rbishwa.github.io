I am currently working as a Project Associate under Prof. Rahul Mangal.I am working on understanding the dynamics of Active Matter in complex media with both experimentation and simulation.I am very much passionate about topics concerning Polymer Physics, Soft Matter and their interplay in colloidal systems in different environments.
I am a B.Tech and M.Tech dual degree graduate in Chemical Engineering from IIT Kanpur.I have also prior experience working in the Optical fibre glass industry and also worked in projects in interdisciplinary fields like Nano-technology & Thin Film deposition.I am very well versed with softwares and languages like MATLAB, COMSOL and Python.
Interests. Soft Matter, Polymer Physics, Active Matter, Thin Film Deposition, Nano-Technology
